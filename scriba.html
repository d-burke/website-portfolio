Scriba

Mobile app that allows users to post and view messages on location.

Features a large GPS map populated with icons of neaby posted messages. Map icon images denotes the message's catagory and tapping the icon brings up the expanded information for that post. Tap the bottom right pencil icon to post your own message to your current GPS location.

The posts page lists messages in your area. List can be sorted by distance from you, most recent, or highest rated. Tapping a message will bring up that message's expanded information page.

Expanded information page shows that massage's location, author, full message test, how long ago it was posted, and its total votes.

Problem This Solves

The need to post a public message without vandalizing or littering.
