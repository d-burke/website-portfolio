Scriba

A mobile app that allows users to post and view messages on location.

Features a large GPS map populated with icons of neaby posted messages. Map icon images denotes the message's catagory and touching the icon brings up the expanded information for that post. Touch the bottom write pencil icon to post your own message to your current GPS location. (Posting requires easy account creation with Auth0 using your Google or Facebook)

Posts page lists posted messages in your area. List can be sorted by proximity, most recent, and highest rated. Touching a message will bring up that message's expanded information page.

Posted message's expanded information page shows the massages map location, author, full message, how long ago it was posted, and total vote count. Users also can vote the message up or down from here.

Problem This Solves

The need to post a public message without vandalizing or littering.
